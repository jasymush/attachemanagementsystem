About Attaches Management System

The Attaches Management System (AMS) is designed to streamline the management of attachees within the Ministry. It provides a centralized platform for attachees to submit reports, complaints, and recommendations, while enabling supervisors and directors to monitor progress and manage departmental information effectively.

  • Role-based User Authentication
  • Attachee Report Submission (Days Absent, Complaints, Recommendations)
  • Supervisor/Director Dashboard for Oversight
  • User-friendly Interface with Theme Customization
  • Secure Data Storage with PostgreSQL and SQLAlchemy

Developed with Flask, HTML, CSS, and Bootstrap.
